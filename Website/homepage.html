Hi everyone! Welcome to my New York City Food Blog! As an avid fan of food, New York is every foodie’s heaven. Every time I posted a story on Instagram of what I was eating, everyone would ask me where are you?! The Food looks amazing! Therefore, I decided to create this food blog to share my experiences with everyone! If you are in need for a recommendation for delicious food, this is the page for you!

My food blog is here to make sure your taste buds explode with flavorful food. The beauty of New York city to be able to try the worlds food without travelling. No other city in the US can serve the authenticity of the delicate of each cuisine, the way New York does. During those hard times during my internship, I would go to Saravana Bhavan and Shamash ((both are Indian restaurants) that would transport me back to India and give me the comfort of being close to home. I try my best to explore as many cultures and cosines as I can,

I know there are so many individuals who are so far away from home, but I promise to try to find the best restaurants that can make you feel a bit closer to home with my food blog.
