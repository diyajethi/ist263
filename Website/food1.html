My Top Three Recommedations!

After A lot of soul searching with the plenty of amazing places in New York, I have found these places as my go to!


Chloe's Soft Fruit Serve

Is that New York summer heat getting to you! Are you looking for a cool and yummy healthier alternative to ice cream, then you have to visit Chloe's Soft Fruit Serve! This is my go to during the summer to combat the heat. They have many different flavours from strawberry,mango,blueberry- all made from real fruits

And Guess What? THEY HAVE UNLIMMITED THE TOPINGS. You can have as much as nutella, granola, fruits as you want!


La Pecora Bianca

The simplest yet most delicious meal you can get - Pasta! But what is so special about Pecora Bianaca that their food is all over everyones instagram story! Its their Burrata Pasta (the picture above). With a spicy vodka sauce and soft burrata melting in your mouth as it adds the cheesiness to the tomato's sauciness.


Bestia

Bestia hands down has the best pizza I have ever had in my life! All their pizzas have a soft buttery crust leaving you looking for more pziza even when you are stuffed. They have delicious margerita, chicken pesto and mushroom truffle pizza! I definelty recommened their mushroom truffle pizza, I mean who doesnt love truffle!

But after all that delicious pizza, we need to end the night with a chocolate haven! Bestia's choclate tart is the definition of a chocolate haven that you do not want to come out of! Smoothy, creamy and undeniably tasty, I literally had a dream about it the other night.
